Name

Sofiia Ulianova

Focus

Artist-Illustrator

Sofiia Ulianova (2022 – ) is a Russian artist and illustrator. After graduating in economics from the Higher School of Economics, Moscow, decided to continue her studies in art and design. In 2020 she graduated web design at Gorbunov Bureau School, in 2022 – illustration at British Higher School of Art and Design.

A playful approach to illustration gives Sofiia’s work a fresh and ironical touch. Her work is well known for funy characters and punchy colours.

Currently she is working as freelance illustrator in the field of editorial, graphic and web design.
